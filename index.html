About Mimi's Delivery

Built for the way Lagos actually moves

Mimi's Delivery started with a simple frustration: too many deliveries in Lagos were "trust me, it's on the way" with no visibility. We built a platform where every package is tracked, every rider is verified, and every price is shown before you commit.

Our mission

To make sending a package across Lagos as easy and predictable as sending a message — with real-time visibility for customers, fair earnings for riders, and full control for operations.

01

Founded in Lagos

Built specifically for Lagos traffic patterns, zones, and delivery realities.

02

Rider-first approach

Verified riders, transparent earnings, and fair job assignment.

03

Real-time everything

Every order status updates live — no refreshing, no guessing.

Pricing

Simple, distance-based pricing

Every delivery is priced using a base fee, distance in kilometers, and your chosen delivery type. No hidden charges.
